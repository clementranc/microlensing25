Single epoch astrometric microlensing in lensed quasars

Raquel Forés-Toribio  ✧  University of Valencia, Spain

Future improvements in astrometric instrumentation will allow centroid measurements of a few microarcseconds. In the scenario of quasar microlensing, such precision could be employed to infer lens systems properties (such as mass fraction in compact objects or quasars disks sizes) in a single campaign of observations. In this work we present how lens parameters such as convergence, shear, compact objects or source sizes affect centroid displacements.
